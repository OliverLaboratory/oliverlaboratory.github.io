Designing algorithms to decode the language of biomolecular structure.

We build structure-aware machine learning methods that process growing datasets of biological structures to uncover the geometric patterns driving function in proteins and RNA, from static structures to conformational dynamics.

Center for AI in Protein Dynamics · Vanderbilt University

Research

Protein Structure & Dynamics

Representing and learning from protein conformational dynamics and 3D structure. Scalable tools for impactful biological prediction tasks.

RNA 3D & Drug Discovery

Representation learning on RNA 3D structures for applications in small-molecule drug discovery and structural motif mining.

Graph Pattern Mining

Approximate network pattern discovery in noisy biological graphs, with applications to pre-training and classification.
